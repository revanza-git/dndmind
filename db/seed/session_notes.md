# Sample Session Notes

Captain Vey betrayed the party at Blackwater Mine. He sold the old royal map to the Ashen Knives and escaped through the smuggler tunnel beneath the collapsed ore lift.

Mira Thorn swore to track Vey down. Orren Vale recovered the Dawn Shard from the flooded chapel, but the relic pulsed when it came near the mine's sealed bronze door.

The party promised Mayor Elowen that they would protect Eldermire before the next new moon. A masked agent watched them from the roof of the Silver Lantern Inn and left a black feather behind.

Unresolved hooks:
- Who paid Captain Vey?
- What does the Dawn Shard unlock?
- Why are the Ashen Knives interested in Eldermire?
